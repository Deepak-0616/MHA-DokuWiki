My Hero Academia (Anime)

My Hero Academia is an anime adaptation of the manga written and illustrated by Kōhei Horikoshi. Produced by Studio Bones, the series follows the journey of Izuku Midoriya in a world where superpowers—known as Quirks—are the norm.

Studio: Bones
First aired: April 3, 2016
Episodes: 150+
Status: Ongoing

Synopsis

Izuku Midoriya dreams of becoming a hero like his idol All Might, despite being born without a Quirk. After a fateful encounter, Izuku inherits the legendary power One For All and enrolls in U.A. High School, where he trains to become a true hero alongside other aspiring students.
