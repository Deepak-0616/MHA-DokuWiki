About DokuWiki

A fan-built archive inspired by the world of My Hero Academia

What is DokuWiki?

DokuWiki is a fan-made project created to document the characters, story arcs, episodes, and evolution of the My Hero Academia universe. It is built as a clean, fast, wiki-style reference — nothing more, nothing less.

Characters

Heroes, villains, students, pros, and legends — organized and searchable.

Episodes

Season-wise episode lists designed for quick browsing and clarity.

Story Arcs

A structured timeline of sagas and arcs that shape the entire story.

Pure HTML & CSS

No frameworks. No JavaScript bloat. Just clean, readable markup.

Why this exists

This project exists to learn, to experiment, and to build something meaningful from scratch. It is intentionally simple — because information should be easy to read, not hard to reach.
