About DokuWiki – My Hero Academia Fan Site

DokuWiki is a fan-made project inspired by the heroic world of My Hero Academia (Boku no Hero Academia). This site collects information about characters, story arcs, episodes, and more from across the series.

The goal of this project is to provide a simple, clean, and easy-to-navigate hub for fans who want to explore the story from the beginning of U.A. High School to the final battles between heroes and villains.

What you’ll find here

  • Character profiles and groups
  • Episode lists separated by seasons
  • Major story arcs and sagas
  • A simple wiki-style layout rebuilt using HTML & CSS only

This site is not official and is not affiliated with Kōhei Horikoshi, Shueisha, or any licensed distributor of My Hero Academia. It is created solely for educational and fan purposes.
