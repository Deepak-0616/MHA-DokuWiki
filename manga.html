My Hero Academia (Manga)

My Hero Academia is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Kōhei Horikoshi. It is the original source material for the anime, films, and spin-off works.

Author: Kōhei Horikoshi
Publisher: Shueisha
Magazine: Weekly Shōnen Jump
Published: July 2014 – August 2024
Volumes: 42
Status: Completed

Overview

The manga tells the story of Izuku Midoriya, a boy born without superpowers in a world where Quirks are common. After meeting his idol All Might, Izuku begins his journey toward becoming a professional hero by enrolling at U.A. High School.

Publication History

The series debuted in Weekly Shōnen Jump in 2014 and quickly gained popularity for its character-driven storytelling and superhero themes. Over its ten-year run, the manga expanded into multiple arcs and sagas, concluding in 2024.
